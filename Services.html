BMI Calculator


BMI Categories:

Underweight = <18.5
Normal weight = 18.5 to 24.9
Overweight = 25 to 29.9
Obesity = BMI of 30 or greater


Body Fat Calculator


General body fat percentage categories:

  • Essential: 9-11% for women and 3-5% for men
  • Below Average/Athletes: 12-19% for women and 6-13% for men
  • General Fitness: 20-24% for women and 14-17% for men
  • Average/Acceptable: 25-29% for women and 18-24% for men
  • Obese (Level I & II): 30% + for women and 25% + for men
